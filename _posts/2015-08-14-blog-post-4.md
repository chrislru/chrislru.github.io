Emotional voice databases
------

Here is a list of emotional voice data I have compiled during the first stage of my project. These datasets contain various emotional speech and non-speech vocal expressions.

* [CREMA-D - (Crowd-sourced Emotional Multimodal Actors Dataset)](https://github.com/CheyneyComputerScience/CREMA-D)
* [Emo-DB - Berlin Database of Emotional Speech](http://emodb.bilderbar.info/index-1280.html)
* [EmoFilm](https://zenodo.org/record/1326428)
